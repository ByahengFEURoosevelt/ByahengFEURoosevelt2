Hello, Tamaraws!

FEURmoris is a student-driven magazine of FEU Roosevelt, celebrating the Tamaraw spirit through stories of creativity, unity, and growth.

This magazine isn’t just about words and images, it’s about us, our growth, and the spirit that unites us as Tamaraws.

TAKING THE NEXT CHAPTER

THE TRANSITION THAT DEFINE US

Every Tamaraw’s story at FEU Roosevelt is a journey of transition, from fear to confidence, silence to expression, uncertainty to purpose. This feature follows seven Tamaraws, each with their own story of growth, resilience, and discovery.

Through friendships, shared challenges, and mentors who believed in them, they learned that transition isn’t just about change — it’s about becoming. Together, they embody the spirit of FEUR: that growth happens not alone, but side by side.

At FEU Roosevelt, every chapter ends with a new beginning — and every Tamaraw carries forward the legacy of courage, connection, and transformation.

THE REBIRTH OF MARKETING

"Taking the Next Chapter the Tamaraw Way"

Before the pandemic, marketing thrived in the energy of face-to-face events, mall activations, and personal connections. Then 2020 arrived — and everything stopped. But while the world went silent, marketers didn’t. They adapted, innovated, and turned screens into stages.

From livestreams to hashtags, marketing found a new way to connect — one built on resilience, creativity, and community. Just like the Tamaraws, who turned their struggles into strength, their isolation into innovation.

This is more than a story about marketing’s evolution — it’s about how the Tamaraw spirit rose above uncertainty, redefining what it means to adapt, inspire, and create in a new era.

FROM CULINARY DREAMS TO MARKETING JOURNEYS:

A STORY OF FAITH, PERSEVERANCE, AND GROWTHS

She once dreamed of becoming a chef, but now she cooks up stories and strategies with the same passion, this time as a marketer. Her FEU Roosevelt journey was far from easy; sleepless nights, endless projects, and the weight of leadership tested her in ways she never imagined.

Through faith, friendship, and fierce determination, she learned that true leadership isn’t measured by titles or achievements. It’s defined by empathy, patience, and heart.

From designing the official SINAG TAMARAW 2023 logo to stepping boldly into her first job, she continues to prove that real growth begins when you choose courage over comfort and never stop believing in your own story.

Mr. Jason Carlos

The Heart That Never Left:

“A Teacher’s Journey Through Time”

From chalkboards to AI — Sir Jason Carlos has seen it all. A true-blooded Rooseveltian, he began his journey as a student in 1991 and has spent nearly 30 years shaping minds at FEU Roosevelt.

His story is one of passion, resilience, and transformation — proving that while tools may change, the heart of teaching remains the same. Discover how one teacher’s legacy continues to inspire generations of Tamaraws.
