Discrete Mathematics

Ms. Maria Luisa Delos Santos

Discrete Mathematics has been an exciting journey for me and my partner. Together, we explored topics like Set Theory, Propositions, Algorithms, Summation, Data Structures, and Relations. At first, some of these concepts seemed abstract and challenging, but working side by side made the learning process more engaging. We spent hours discussing problems, creating our own examples, and helping each other understand the logic behind each topic. Set Theory taught us how to organize and categorize information, while Propositions helped us sharpen our reasoning skills. Working on Algorithms and Summations enhanced our problem-solving abilities, and studying Relations showed us the connections between different mathematical structures. Through this subject, not only did we gain a deeper appreciation for mathematics, but we also strengthened our teamwork, patience, and persistence, making each challenge a memorable learning experience.
