Red Cross CPR Training and Disaster Preparedness Seminar

September 3, 2025

The Red Cross CPR training and Disaster Preparedness seminar held at FEU Roosevelt Cainta was an engaging and insightful experience for everyone involved. The seminar began with discussions led by Red Cross representatives who highlighted the importance of readiness and presence of mind during emergencies. They explained how preparedness can greatly reduce panic and damage during disasters like earthquakes, typhoons, and fires. The session also emphasized the role of individuals in promoting safety within their communities and the value of knowing basic life-saving techniques such as CPR. During the CPR training portion, participants learned how to perform Cardiopulmonary Resuscitation through a creative and resourceful approach—using water gallons as mannequins. Even without actual CPR dummies, the instructors demonstrated proper hand placement, compression depth, and rhythm, allowing everyone to simulate the procedure effectively. This improvisation made the activity both fun and educational, showing that life-saving skills can be practiced with available materials. The experience helped participants understand the importance of timing, pressure, and confidence when performing CPR in real-life situations.

Cyber Crime and Anti-Hazing Seminar

September 24, 2025

The Cyber Crime and Anti-Hazing Seminar held at FEU Roosevelt Marikina Campus was an eye-opening and educational experience that emphasized the importance of awareness, responsibility, and safety both online and in real life. The speakers discussed various forms of cybercrime, such as online scams, identity theft, and cyberbullying, reminding students to protect their personal information and use technology wisely. They also explained the Cybercrime Prevention Act of 2012 and the Anti-Hazing Law (Republic Act No. 11053), highlighting the serious consequences of violating these laws. The seminar provided valuable lessons on respecting others, promoting safe digital practices, and rejecting any form of violence or coercion in organizations. Overall, the event encouraged students to be responsible digital citizens and advocates of a safe, respectful community.

Sinag Sports 2025 & Cosplay Event

October 28, 2025

The Sinag Sports 2025 and Cosplay Event at FEU Roosevelt was a vibrant celebration of talent, creativity, and school spirit that united students from both the Cainta and Marikina campuses. The sports events showcased the athletic skills, teamwork, and determination of the participants, fostering friendly competition and camaraderie among the Tamaraw community. Mental Health Awareness Seminar

November 5, 2025

The Mental Health Awareness Seminar held at FEU Roosevelt Marikina Campus was an insightful and meaningful event that aimed to promote understanding, empathy, and self-care among students. The seminar featured discussions led by mental health advocates and professionals who emphasized the importance of emotional well-being, stress management, and seeking help when needed. Students learned about recognizing early signs of anxiety, depression, and burnout, as well as practical ways to maintain a healthy mindset despite academic and personal challenges. The event encouraged open conversations about mental health, breaking the stigma surrounding it and reminding everyone that it’s okay to ask for support. Overall, the seminar fostered a safe and supportive environment that inspired students to prioritize their mental wellness and look out for one another.
